COMMON INQUIRIES

Do you offer wholesale partnerships?

Yes. We work with select retailers who align with our empire aesthetic. Minimum order quantities and terms are discussed individually. Contact us via the wholesale inquiry option above.

Can I request a custom/bespoke piece?

Bespoke orders are available for select clients. Lead time is 4-6 weeks depending on complexity. Contact us with your vision and measurements.

How do I track my order?

All orders receive tracking information via WhatsApp and email within 24 hours of shipment. You can also contact us directly for real-time updates.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unworn items with original tags. Custom/bespoke pieces are final sale. See our full policy for details.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within Pakistan only. International shipping is planned for Q3 2026. Join our mailing list for updates.

How can I collaborate with DRIP?

We're open to collaborations with artists, designers, and brands that share our vision. Submit a detailed proposal using the collaboration inquiry option.

THE DOSSIER

RETURN PROTOCOL

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached. This is strict. We do not accept returns on used gear.

To initiate a return, contact us via WhatsApp with your Order ID.

PAYMENT METHODS

Cash on Delivery (COD) is available nationwide. Payment is collected upon successful delivery of your order.

Online transfers are available upon request via our support channel.

DATA PRIVACY
