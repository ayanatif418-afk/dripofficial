CHAPTER II

THE INDUSTRIAL METHOD

MATERIAL SELECTION

We source only heavyweight fabrics exceeding 300 GSM. Each textile is tested for durability, weight, and drape. Only the finest materials make it to the cutting table.

PATTERN ENGINEERING

Our patterns are architectural blueprints. Every measurement is calculated for the Empire fit—oversized without being loose, structured without being rigid.

MANUAL FINISHING

Acid washes are done by hand. Distressing is individual. No two pieces are identical. This is where mass production ends and craft begins.

QUALITY INSPECTION

Every piece undergoes final inspection. Stitching, weight, finish, and fit are verified. Only garments that meet the Empire standard receive the DRIP label.
